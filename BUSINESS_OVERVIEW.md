## ğŸ“± Core Workflows (What Users Do)

### 1. **Login**
- Administrator logs in with email/password
- Can also use Google or Apple login
- Secure access to tournament data

### 2. **Create New Tournament** (5-Step Setup)

#### **Step 1: Competition Setup**
- Name the tournament
- Choose division (Premier, Division 1, 2, etc.)
- Set game rules:
  - Number of games in tournament
  - Number of quarters (usually 4)
  - Quarter duration (e.g., 10 minutes)
  - Overtime duration
- Pick tournament date, time, venue
- Upload tournament flyer/poster
- Assign officials (Crew Chief, Umpires, Commissioner)

#### **Step 2: Team Setup**
- Add all participating teams
- Each team has:
  - Team name and color
  - Unique team code
  - Team logo
  - Coaching staff (Coach, Assistant Coaches)
  - Country/State location

#### **Step 3: Player Management**
- Add players to each team
- Assign jersey numbers
- Upload player photos
- Set height/position info

#### **Step 4: Review**
- See all tournament details before going live

#### **Step 5: Complete**
- Finalize and launch tournament

### 3. **Dashboard - Game Overview**

Users see at a glance:
- **Ongoing Game** - Current live score (e.g., Team A 17 vs Team B 12)
- **Up Next** - Games coming up with times and venues
- **Recent Results** - Scores from past games
- **Upcoming Tournaments** - Tournaments being organized

### 4. **View Tournament Details**

- See all teams and their standings
- View match fixtures
- Check game schedules
- See pending games not yet played
- View tournament leaders (top scorers)
- Copy tournament code to share with others

### 5. **Track Live Match**

During a game:
- Real-time score entry (quarter by quarter)
- Track individual player statistics:
  - Points scored
  - Rebounds
  - Assists
  - Blocks
  - Steals
- View box score (detailed player stats)
- **Shot Chart** - Visual representation of where players took shots (made vs missed)

### 6. **Player Details**

Click on any player to see:
- Full game statistics
- Performance trends
- Shot chart for that player
- Comparison to other players

### 7. **View Results**

- Browse all past match results
- Filter by date
- See final scores
- Search for specific teams or matches

### 8. **Manage Statisticians**

- Register official statisticians
- Keep their profiles (location, contact, bio)
- Assign to tournaments
- View statistician details
